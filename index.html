Time Wasters

A Collection Of Little Games To Play When You Are Bored

Grid Guardians

This Is A Fun Little Game Where You Have To Press The Right Key At The Right Time To Defeat The Zombies.

How To Play
Use Arrow keys or WASD to attack the zombies in the lane of that direction

Use P to Pause
Use T to toggle effects (May decrease any lag!)
You can use SPACE to quick restart when on the menu!



Grapple Pig

a short, 200 second game where you play as a pig,
trying to collect as much treasure as possible before the time is up.

How To Play
Click or Tap - Throw Grappling Hook
Arrow Keys or W A S D - Run and Jump

Attack enemies by throwing your grappling hook at them.



Neon Dodge

try and get the best high score by collecting coins, and dodging the red cubes.

How To Play
Use the mouse pointer to move around
Collect coins for extra points
Avoid the red cubes
